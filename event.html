About the Event

Join the Rotaract Club of New LJIET for our annual Blood Donation Drive, a cornerstone event in our commitment to community health and service. Every year, our community faces a shortage of blood, and your single donation can be a lifeline for someone in critical need. This event is organized in partnership with the local Red Cross chapter to ensure the highest standards of safety and care for all donors. We believe that small acts of kindness can create waves of positive change. Come, be a hero, and help us make a tangible impact on the lives of people in our community.
