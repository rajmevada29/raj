About Our Club

The Rotaract Club of New LJIET is a community of young leaders dedicated to service and professional development. Our mission is to provide an opportunity for young men and women to enhance the knowledge and skills that will assist them in personal development, to address the physical and social needs of their communities, and to promote better relations between all people worldwide through a framework of friendship and service.
